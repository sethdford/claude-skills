---
description: Run a complete sprint planning process from capacity to commitment.
argument-hint: [team size, sprint length, business priorities]
---

Chain these steps:

1. Use the `capacity-planning` skill to calculate available team capacity (account for meetings, support, unknowns)
2. Use the `estimation-techniques` skill to establish estimation baseline for your team's context
3. Use the `sprint-planning-guide` skill to facilitate the planning meeting and build the sprint goal
4. Use the `dependency-mapping` skill to identify any cross-team blockers or sequencing constraints
5. Synthesize findings into a committed sprint backlog with sprint goal and success criteria

After completion, suggest:

- How to track velocity against commitment (use `velocity-analysis` for future sprints)
- Which risky items need spike/investigation tasks before next sprint
- Whether any technical debt should be addressed alongside feature work
