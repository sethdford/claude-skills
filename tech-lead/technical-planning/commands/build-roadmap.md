---
description: Create a 3-12 month technical roadmap aligned with business strategy.
argument-hint: [business priorities, team constraints, technical debt inventory]
---

Chain these steps:

1. Use the `technical-roadmap` skill to structure the roadmap narrative and define strategic themes
2. Use the `capacity-planning` skill to model capacity across quarters and identify hiring needs
3. Use the `work-breakdown-structure` skill to decompose major initiatives into epics with ownership
4. Use the `dependency-mapping` skill to identify critical path items and cross-team blockers
5. Use the `technical-debt-prioritization` skill to reserve capacity for debt paydown and identify which debt blocks roadmap items
6. Synthesize findings into a 2-3 page roadmap document with quarterly milestones, dependencies, and narrative on business impact

After completion, suggest:

- How to communicate roadmap to stakeholders and manage expectations
- What gates/decisions must happen before each quarter starts
- How to run quarterly reviews and adapt the roadmap as conditions change
