---
description: Create team charter defining working agreements, values, and norms.
argument-hint: [team composition, team stage, known dysfunction]
---

Chain these steps:

1. Use the `team-charter` skill to facilitate charter creation with team input
2. Define working agreements: meeting norms, communication expectations, conflict resolution
3. Define team values: what matters most to this team?
4. Synthesize into charter document and commit to team wiki

After completion, suggest:

- How to make charter living (review and evolve quarterly)
- How to reference charter when issues arise
- How to celebrate charter successes
