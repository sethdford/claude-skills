---
description: Create and structure a mentoring relationship with clear goals and milestones.
argument-hint: [mentor/mentee pair, skill gaps, career goals]
---

Chain these steps:

1. Use the `one-on-one-template` skill to establish regular mentoring cadence
2. Use the `mentoring-plan` skill to define goals and milestones (3-6 month timeframe)
3. Use the `skill-matrix` skill to identify specific skills to build
4. Use the `career-ladder-design` skill to connect mentoring goals to career progression
5. Synthesize into a mentoring agreement with goals, meeting cadence, and success criteria

After completion, suggest:

- How to structure mentoring sessions (tech discussions, code review focus, project guidance)
- How to measure progress and adjust goals
- How to celebrate achievements and course-correct if needed
