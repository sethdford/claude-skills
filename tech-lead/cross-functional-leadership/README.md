# Cross-Functional Leadership Plugin

Partner effectively with product, design, and stakeholders to deliver aligned outcomes.

## Skills (7 skills)

- **technical-product-partnership** — Build trusting relationships with PMs by showing up early in planning
- **design-engineering-handoff** — Create smooth handoffs from design to implementation
- **stakeholder-communication** — Keep stakeholders informed and manage expectations
- **cross-team-coordination** — Coordinate work across multiple teams with clear dependencies
- **technical-requirements-translation** — Translate technical constraints and risks into business language
- **scope-negotiation** — Work with product to find viable scope when requirements exceed capacity
- **alignment-facilitation** — Run meetings that drive shared understanding across functions

## Commands (3 commands)

- **align-teams** — Facilitate alignment between engineering and other functions
- **translate-requirements** — Convert technical constraints into business impact
- **negotiate-scope** — Negotiate achievable scope when capacity is constrained

## Key Principles

1. Tech leads are translators between technical reality and business needs
2. Collaborate early: weigh in on requirements before they're finalized
3. Manage expectations: be clear about capacity, risks, and trade-offs
4. Show empathy: understand product and design challenges, not just engineering challenges
5. Build trust: follow through on commitments and communicate proactively
