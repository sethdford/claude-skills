# Discovery Plugin

Problem discovery, customer research, and opportunity identification for product managers.

## Skills (10)

1. **problem-statement** — Define a clear, evidence-based problem statement without solution bias
2. **customer-interview-guide** — Design and conduct structured customer interviews
3. **opportunity-assessment** — Evaluate problem size, urgency, and addressability
4. **assumption-mapping** — Document and prioritize strategy assumptions for validation
5. **customer-segmentation** — Identify and prioritize distinct customer segments
6. **competitive-landscape** — Map competitors and alternative solutions
7. **market-sizing** — Estimate TAM, SAM, and SOM with supporting data
8. **pain-point-analysis** — Systematically identify and rank customer pain points
9. **voice-of-customer** — Aggregate feedback across channels into actionable insights
10. **discovery-sprint-plan** — Plan and execute focused 1-week discovery sprints

## Commands (4)

1. **run-discovery** — Execute complete discovery sprint to identify and validate problems
2. **interview-customers** — Design and conduct structured customer interviews
3. **size-opportunity** — Quantify market size and financial opportunity
4. **map-assumptions** — Document and prioritize riskiest strategy assumptions

## Key Concepts

- **Evidence-based discovery**: Ground all insights in customer data, not intuition
- **Problem vs. solution**: Validate the problem before proposing solutions
- **Customer segmentation**: Tailor discovery to distinct customer groups
- **Opportunity quantification**: Size markets realistically with TAM/SAM/SOM
- **Assumption validation**: Test critical assumptions cheaply before major investment
- **Continuous learning**: Discovery is ongoing, not a phase-gate process
