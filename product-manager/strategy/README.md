# Strategy Plugin

Product vision, strategy, positioning, and go-to-market planning.

## Skills (8)

1. **product-vision** — Articulate compelling long-term vision that inspires teams
2. **product-strategy** — Define strategic direction, key bets, and trade-offs
3. **positioning-statement** — Craft market positioning that resonates with customers
4. **value-proposition** — Define specific value delivered relative to alternatives
5. **business-model-canvas** — Map how value is created, delivered, and captured
6. **product-market-fit** — Define and measure PMF indicators for your segment
7. **strategic-narrative** — Build compelling narrative connecting vision to market
8. **okr-definition** — Translate strategy into measurable OKRs

## Commands (3)

1. **define-strategy** — Develop comprehensive product strategy
2. **write-vision** — Create inspiring product vision
3. **set-okrs** — Translate strategy into executable OKRs

## Key Concepts

- **Vision-driven strategy**: Strategy operationalizes vision
- **Clear trade-offs**: Success means saying no to some things
- **Customer-centric positioning**: Grounded in customer outcomes, not features
- **Measurable goals**: OKRs bridge strategy and execution
- **Coherent narrative**: Strategy story resonates across audiences

- *Playing to Win* (A.G. Lafley, Roger Martin)
- *Blue Ocean Strategy* (Kim & Mauborgne)
