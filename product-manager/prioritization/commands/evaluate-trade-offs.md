Chain these steps:

1. Use the `impact-effort-matrix` skill to visualize trade-offs
2. Use the `cost-of-delay` skill to quantify delay impacts
3. Use the `opportunity-scoring` skill to assess market opportunities
4. Use the `weighted-scoring` skill to make final ranking decision

After completion, document decision rationale and share with team.
